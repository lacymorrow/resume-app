Four directions for the résumé

Static stubs, not finished pages. Each one takes the same idea — one canonical résumé that re-renders itself per role — and gives it a different visual personality. Pick and choose: a direction, or elements across directions.

  1. 01 Broadsheet Editorial print. Serif typography, hairline rules, oxblood accent. Flavors as newspaper “editions.” Doubles as the printable résumé.
  2. 02 Manpage The résumé as man lacymorrow. One mono family, warm amber on charcoal, flavors as command flags. The Lacy Shell product story.
  3. 03 Swiss International Typographic Style. Huge grotesque type, numbered sections, tabular experience rows. Optimized for the 30-second recruiter scan.
  4. 04 Spectrum Dark studio portfolio. Each flavor has its own hue and re-tints the whole page — the switcher becomes the design idea itself.

Content is stubbed from the real resume.json; flavor switchers demo 3–4 flavors each. Nothing here ships as-is.
